The floor, per unit, over the hold: depreciation (value lost) + tax & title to sell + intercompany fee + maintenance, spread over the months held. That's the rent needed just to cover costs — break even, no profit. Heads-up: the intercompany fee is money one LLC you own pays another you own — a real cost to the rental LLC, but it nets out at the combined-owner level, so don't read it as a true loss when you look at the whole business.
