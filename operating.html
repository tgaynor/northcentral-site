No. 04 — Pilotage

Built one worth buying. Now I help a few founders do the same.

I bootstrapped Anavate from a garage to an eight-figure exit — no investors, no investment bank — and sold it to a PE-backed strategic. These days I work hands-on with a small number of growing businesses, doing the unglamorous work that moves revenue and builds real enterprise value.

How it works

A monthly retainer. We work directly — regular working sessions on your real deals, hires, and decisions, plus access between sessions when something can't wait. No decks for the sake of decks. I take on a limited number of companies at a time so each one gets real attention.

Who this is for

Founder-led businesses doing real revenue and ready to grow — home services, hospitality, SaaS, and professional or IT services. You're past product-market fit and the constraint is now go-to-market: you're the bottleneck, the deals are getting bigger or messier, and you want the business to be worth something beyond your own hustle.

What I do
01
Get you out of selling.
I closed eight figures myself, then built the team that took it off my plate. I'll find what's actually working in your sales, write it down so someone else can run it, make the first sales hires (and help you avoid the expensive wrong ones), and set up comp so revenue doesn't live and die on you being in the room.
